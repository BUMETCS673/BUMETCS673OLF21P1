Cheffy is a recipe app that allows you to enter the ingredients you have on hand and to search for meals that can be made from them.

The motivation behind this application is to help you discover new recipes, and to reduce food waste by finding you ingredients that might otherwise go to waste.

In addition, you can save favorite recipes into your account or create a personal pantry that can help you enter ingredients more easily.

Come and enjoy Cheffy!
